Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08 September 2025

PinAI Studio (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a SaaS tool that helps users generate AI images and publish them to their own Pinterest accounts. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & OAuth Data: When you connect Pinterest, we receive an access token and allowed scopes (e.g., pins:write, boards:read). We never receive your Pinterest password.
  • Content You Create: Prompts, settings, and the AI images you choose to generate and/or upload.
  • Usage & Device Data: Basic diagnostics (pages used, timestamps, rough device/browser info) to improve reliability.
  • Support Info: Messages or email you send us.

2) How We Use Information

  • To let you create, preview, and publish pins to your Pinterest boards.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the service (including safety/abuse prevention).
  • To provide support and communicate important updates.

3) Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We share data only with subprocessors necessary to run the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under confidentiality and data-processing terms.
  • We may disclose if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

4) Tokens & Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely (encrypted at rest) and scoped to the minimum required.
  • You can revoke access anytime in your Pinterest settings; our app will stop working until reconnected.
  • We use HTTPS everywhere and restrict internal access on a need-to-know basis.

5) Data Retention

  • AI images, prompts, and logs are retained only as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.
  • Upon account disconnect or deletion request, associated tokens are promptly invalidated and scheduled for removal from our systems.

6) Your Rights & Choices

  • Access/Correction/Deletion: Contact us to request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Revoke Pinterest Access: Manage connected apps in Pinterest to immediately disable our access.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of non-essential analytics by contacting us.

7) International Users & Legal Bases

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process personal data under GDPR legal bases such as contract (to provide the service) and legitimate interests (to secure and improve the service). You may lodge a complaint with your local authority.

8) Children’s Privacy

PinAI Studio is not directed to children under 13 (or the age of digital consent where you live). We do not knowingly collect such data.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email.
